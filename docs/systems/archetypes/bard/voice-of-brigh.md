## Replacement details

### Replaces: fascinate

- **Archetype feature:** Bardic Performance
- **Description:** A Voice of Brigh gains the following bardic performances.
- **Mechanics:**
  - Type: Su.
  - Level hooks: 8, 12, 14.
  - Mechanics summary: A Voice of Brigh gains the following bardic performances. Brigh’s Soothing (Su) : A Voice of Brigh can use Brigh’s calming voice to pacify constructs. This functions as the fascinate bardic performance, except it can target only constructs. Brigh’s Anger (Su) : At 8th level, a Voice of Brigh can use Brigh’s enraged voice to instill constructs with a sense of dread. This functions as the dirge of doom bardic performance, except it can target only constructs. Brigh’s Spark (Su) : At 12th level, a Voice of Brigh can use Brigh’s animating spark to reanimate a destroyed construct.
- **Implementation flags:**
  - Likely existing hooks: rage/rage-power hook, bardic performance hook.
